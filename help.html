Get Help

If you or someone you know is struggling with addiction, please reach out for help.

Contact local resources or call a helpline for support.

  • Alcohol and Drug Information Service: 1300 13 1340 (8.30 am to 10.00 pm every day)
  • Drinkline is the national alcohol helpline: 300 123 1110 (weekdays 9am to 8pm, weekends 11am to 4pm)

    • Student Helps

    You can get help by either asking a teacher, your parents, or a counselor

    Contact your school's student support center or call your local police department.

  • Your school's student support center: (555) 555-5555
